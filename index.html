How it works

Three simple steps

From connecting your account to publishing — you stay in control the entire time.

1

Connect your TikTok account

Authorize Gerador de Vídeo Pro through TikTok's official, secure login flow. You can revoke access anytime.

2

Generate or upload your video

Create a video with AI or upload your own, then set the caption, title and privacy options.

3

Publish to your account

With your confirmation, the video is published directly to your own TikTok account.

Your data

How we use your TikTok data

We only access what is needed to connect your account and publish videos you choose to post.

👤

Basic profile

We read your basic profile (open id, name, avatar) via user.info.basic only to show which account is connected.

⬆️

Publishing videos

We use video.publish to upload the videos you choose to your own TikTok account. Every post is confirmed by you.

🔒

No selling, no sharing

We never sell or share your data. Tokens are stored securely and you can revoke access anytime.
