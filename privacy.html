Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how AI Video Studio ("we", "us", "the app") collects, uses, stores and protects user ("you") information when you use our services, including the integration with the TikTok platform through its official APIs.

1. Information We Collect

When you connect your TikTok account to our app, we may collect:

  • Basic profile information (username, account identifier, profile picture) provided by the TikTok API;
  • Access and refresh tokens required to authorize actions on your behalf;
  • Video content, captions, titles and metadata that you choose to publish or manage through the app;
  • Basic technical data (usage logs, error logs) for the operation and security of the service.

2. How We Use Information

  • To authenticate and authorize your account on the TikTok platform;
  • To publish, schedule and manage video content as requested by you;
  • To operate, maintain and improve the functioning of the app;
  • To ensure security and prevent misuse.

We do not sell, rent or trade your personal data.

3. Data Sharing

We share information only with the TikTok platform (through its official APIs) to the extent necessary to perform the actions you request. We do not share your data with third parties for advertising purposes. We may disclose information if required by law or court order.

4. Storage and Retention

Access tokens are stored securely and used only while your account is connected. You may revoke access at any time, which results in the deletion of the associated tokens. We retain data only for as long as necessary to provide the service or as required by law.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Disconnect your TikTok account and revoke access at any time;
  • Request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data;
  • Obtain information about how your data is processed.

You can also manage or revoke permissions directly in your TikTok account settings.

6. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss or alteration. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The date of the last update will always be shown at the top of this page.
